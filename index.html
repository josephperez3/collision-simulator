Collision Simulator

Play around with collisions!

  • To start, press the start button
  • If you would like to simulate another collision, press the reset button
  • Customize your collision by toggling 2D/1D and elastic/inelastic collisions at the bottom right
  • The status of the colliison type is in the top left
  • Drag the balls to move their position
  • Drag the dragger to change the ball's direction
  • At the top right, you can see the current status of the balls
  • You can also change the velocities and mass of the balls with the sliders
